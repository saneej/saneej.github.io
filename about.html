About Me

Personal Info

I am a passionate instinctive photographer .To me, photography is an art of observation and I find happiness in the little and not so little images that I capture. I don't photograph subjects but the way they make me feel.I am fortunate to be able to spend a good amount of time on my personal projects,traveling and collabrating with an excellent team. I want the world to realize that photographers are simply artists with a camera and a secret eye that captures moments that matter the most.
